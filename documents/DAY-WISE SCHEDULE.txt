DAY-WISE SCHEDULE

* DAY-1:
   * Analyze Problem Statement
   * Construct a draft of Functional-Requirements
   * Construct a draft of Database-Schema
   * Complete building of Authentication and Authorization part of users(based on roles)
   * Learning to work with document-storage.
* DAY-2:
   * Building the common CRUD functionality(where ever required)

         * Read and acknowledge the policies
         * Uploading the documents
         * Downloading the materials posted by HR
      * HR:
         * On-boarding interns.
         * Viewing the progress of the interns
         * Verifying the uploaded documents and sending mails to the respective interns regarding any issues.
      * Manager:
         * View onboarding status of their interns
         * Verify and approve those documents that require MANAGER-approval.
         * See whether there are any issues raised by their interns and respond to those complaints.
